Build a creative agency website in 4 steps

How to create an agency website: Step-by-step guide:

If you work in a creative agency, your website needs to be creative. Many agencies flex on creativity within the same overall framework, or sitemap, based on the information they need to present.

This information is typically the agency mantra or area of focus, historical portfolio work, the team members that execute said portfolio work, and areas for contact form submissions and office locations, etc.

What are the 4 steps of web design?

The process can become repeatable and reliable for every project. Our design process consists of four steps:

Phase 1: Discovery + Strategy:

The first phase of the web design process is Discovery + Strategy. This is where the project begins, and the goals and priorities are set.

  • Serious and professional 
  • Casual and quirky
  • Moody, vintage 
  • Minimalistic
  • Humorous and informal 

Phase 2: Direction + Alignment:

After the Discovery + Strategy phase, the designer has a understanding of the client's needs, goals, and target audience.

The next step is to use this information to create a clear direction for the website's design and development.

Phase 3: Design + Refinement:

The third phase of the web design process is Design + Refinement. In this phase, the web designer takes the direction and alignment established in the previous phase and begins to create the actual visual design of the website.

“Communication is key for website design. This helps design and development stay on track and on budget, and ship the site on time. It's important to knows project goals right from the beginning."

- Walker John, Founder of Frado

In Conclusion:

The 4 stages of the web design process are crucial to ensuring the success of any website. Each stage plays an important role in the design and final development of the website and requires constant collaboration between the designer and the client.

By investing in high-quality design, effective communication, and quality content and images, a website can be a one-of-a-kind experience for users.

Saraj Alex

CEO of RoyexLeaf Organics
